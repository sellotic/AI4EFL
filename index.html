What is AI4EFL 2025?

It is the redesign of the training proposal that incorporates advanced artificial intelligence (AI) tools to facilitate the adaptive learning of essay writing in English as a foreign language (EFL). This new version is built on the pilot carried out in 2024, where 50 English pedagogy students participated in a six-session intervention, using various digital AI tools to guide and improve their writing process.

Methodology and Improvements

A hybrid and autonomous approach

The methodology seeks to promote student autonomy and individualized follow-up according to their needs and level. It combines face-to-face sessions to take a pre-test and post-test, along with guided asynchronous activities that allow for flexible and self-paced learning.

Key Improvements in Version 2

  • Adapted rubrics for differentiated assessment.
  • Inclusion of video tutorials to support asynchronous learning.
  • Strategic use of specific tools for each stage of the essay.

Recommendations for Teachers

It is recommended to focus the proposal as a tool to promote more personalized learning, supported by state-of-the-art technology. This methodology guides students at each stage of writing, provides immediate feedback, and fosters the development of both language skills and critical thinking.
